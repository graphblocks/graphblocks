# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in GraphBlocks a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Show empathy and respect for different viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the technical work.
- Take responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.

Harassment, personal attacks, sexualized attention, intimidation, deliberate
disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not
acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's GitHub maintainer or
organization contact mechanisms. Do not open a public issue containing
sensitive details. Project maintainers will investigate promptly, protect the
privacy and safety of reporters, and apply a response proportionate to the
behavior, up to removal from project spaces.

This policy is based on the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
